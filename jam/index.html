Wedding Jam

Background

Most wedding favors are cute trinkets that people order in bulk and seem very impersonal. Sure it may have the name of the bride and groom printed on it, but are you really going to hold onto that magnet or paperweight? We wanted something that was deeply personal and that you would enjoy as a consumable. As wedding favors, we made you jam! We started by picking fruit together, cutting and processing it, combining all the raw ingredients, and canning it into 8 oz jars. We'll have four different flavors to choose from and there will be a set sampler jars at each table for you to try before you choose which flavor to take home with you. Of course there'll only be so many of each flavor at the table so you'll have to negotiate with your neighbors!
